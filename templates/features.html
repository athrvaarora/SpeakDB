Show me sales by region for the last quarter, sorted by total revenue

Generated SQL:

SELECT 
  region, 
  SUM(order_total) as total_revenue
FROM 
  orders
WHERE 
  order_date >= DATE_SUB(CURRENT_DATE, INTERVAL 3 MONTH)
GROUP BY 
  region
ORDER BY 
  total_revenue DESC;
