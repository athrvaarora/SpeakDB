Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pricing and plans

How does the daily query limit work?

The free plan includes 10 natural language queries per day. The counter resets at midnight UTC. The Enterprise plan has no limit on the number of queries you can make.

Can I cancel my Enterprise subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your Enterprise subscription at any time from your profile page. Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period, and you won't be charged again. After that, your account will automatically revert to the Free plan.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover. Payment processing is handled securely by Stripe.

Is there a difference in the quality of generated queries between plans?

No, both plans use the same advanced AI model for generating database queries from natural language. The difference is in the number of daily queries, supported database types, and additional features like schema exploration and advanced visualizations.

Do you offer discounts for annual billing?

Not currently, but we're considering adding annual billing options with a discount in the future. Stay tuned for updates!

What if I need more than what's offered in the Enterprise plan?

For organizations with specific needs beyond what's covered in our Enterprise plan, please contact us at enterprise@speakdb.com to discuss custom solutions.
